Bio

I am currently working as a professor in the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Guizhou University , China and the leader of the Intelligent Systems Security Research Group. Prior to that, I obtained my Ph.D in Control Science and Engineering from Zhejiang University, China on June 2020. My main research interests are security issues of Cyber-physical systems and indoor positioning and navigation system design, supervised by Prof. Sherman Shen and Prof. Jiming Chen, Prof. Peng Cheng. I have visited University of Technology and Design, Singapore as a visiting PhD student from October 2018 to October 2019 under the supervision of Prof. David K. Y. Yau

If you are interested in working with me, please feel free to drop me an email with your CV.

Research Areas
